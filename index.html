Welcome to op hell :)

  • The op functor goes from Cat to Cat.
  • The op functor on Delta swaps the arrows width-wise and reverses the ordering.
  • The dual of the Grothendieck is not obtained by using C op, you have to also compose with the op functor.
  • There are three ways to op a 2-category:
    1. Reverse the 1-cells but not the 2-cells.
    2. Reverse the 2-cells but not the 1-cells.
    3. Reverse both.
